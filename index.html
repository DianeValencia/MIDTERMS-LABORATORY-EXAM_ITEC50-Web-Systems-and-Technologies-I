Tyson Ngo, better known as "TenZ", is one of the most electrifying professional Valorant players. Former CS:GO prodigy turned Valorant superstar, TenZ is celebrated for his insane aim, quick reflexes, and humble personality. A fan favorite across esports. He's now retired from professional scene and continued his streaming and content creation career.
