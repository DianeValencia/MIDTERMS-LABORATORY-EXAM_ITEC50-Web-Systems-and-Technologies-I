ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

PROFESSIONAL CAREER:
CS:GO
↪ Joined Cloud9 as a professional player in 2019.
↪ Benched in October 2019 due to team underperformance.
Valorant
↪ Transitioned to Valorant in April 2020, joining Cloud9.
↪ Took a break from professional Valorant in January 2021 to focus on content creation.
↪ Loaned to Sentinels in April 2021, following the suspension of Sinatraa.
↪ Contract bought out by Sentinels in June 2021.
↪ Retired from professional Valorant in September 2024.
ACHIEVEMENTS:
↪ VCT Stage 2 Masters Reykjavík Champion (2021)
↪ VCT Masters Madrid Champion (2024)
↪ VCT Masters Reykjavík Grand Finals MVP (2021)
↪ VCT Americas Kickoff Champion (2024)
↪ First North American player to win a VCT Masters Event more than once.
STREAMING AND CONTENT CREATION:
↪ Popular Twitch streamer and YouTuber known for engaging gameplay and tutorials.
↪ Has built a large following, sharing insights into his gaming experiences and personal life.
↪ Creates content on YouTube.
PERSONAL LIFE:
↪ Engaged to Kyedae Shymko, also a content creator and gamer.
↪ Faced significant personal challenges, including Kyedae's cancer diagnosis, leading him to step back from professional gaming temporarily.
↪ Advocates for mental health awareness and the importance of support systems in gaming as he live with ADHD. ↪ Moved to Los Angeles in January 2022.
NOTABLE TRAITS:
↪ Exceptional aim and game sense.
↪ Strong mental resilience developed through personal and professional challenges.
↪ Charismatic personality that resonates with fans and followers.
TRIVIAS:
↪ Tenz is of Vietnamese and French descent.
↪ Tenz was the first player to reach Valorant(now called Radiant) rank in North America during the Closed Beta.
↪ Tenz is protanomaly colorblind.
↪ First North American player to win a VCT Masters Event more than once, being VCT 2021 Stage 2 Masters Reykjavík and VCT 2024 Masters Madrid.
